History

Welcome to Rivertree Rafting Adventures, where the thrill of the rapids meets the tranquility of nature. Established in 1998 by a group of passionate outdoor enthusiasts, Rivertree has been crafting unforgettable rafting experiences for adventurers of all ages and skill levels. Nestled in the heart of the majestic Blue Ridge Mountains, our company was born from a love of exploration and a desire to share the breathtaking beauty of our local rivers. Since our inception, we have remained dedicated to providing safe, exhilarating, and environmentally-conscious adventures that leave a lasting impression on all who embark with us.

At Rivertree, we pride ourselves on delivering more than just a ride down the river – we offer a gateway to adventure, connection, and unforgettable memories. Whether you're seeking the rush of navigating challenging rapids or the serenity of drifting along peaceful waters, our expert guides are here to ensure an experience tailored to your desires. With state-of-the-art equipment, unparalleled knowledge of the region's waterways, and a commitment to sustainability, Rivertree Rafting Adventures invites you to join us for the journey of a lifetime. Come explore the untamed beauty of nature, forge lasting friendships, and discover the thrill of the unknown with Rivertree – where every ripple tells a story, and every adventure leaves you craving more.
