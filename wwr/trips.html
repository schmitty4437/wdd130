Featured Trips

Family Rapids

Join us on an unforgettable family adventure down the gentle rapids of the river. Perfect for families with children of all ages, our Family Rapids trip combines excitement with safety, ensuring everyone has a fantastic time on the water. Experienced guides will lead you through stunning landscapes while providing interesting insights into the local flora and fauna. Create lasting memories as you navigate the river together, splashing through waves and enjoying the thrill of rafting as a family.

Raft & Hike

Embark on an immersive journey that combines the best of rafting and hiking experiences. Our Raft & Hike trip offers the perfect blend of adrenaline-pumping rapids and serene nature walks. Begin your adventure by navigating exciting rapids, feeling the rush of the water as you paddle downstream. Afterward, venture into the heart of the wilderness on a guided hike, exploring hidden trails, cascading waterfalls, and breathtaking viewpoints. This trip is ideal for outdoor enthusiasts seeking an active and scenic adventure.

Wild Rapid Party

Get ready for an adrenaline-fueled party on the wild rapids! Our Wild Rapid Party trip is designed for thrill-seekers looking for an exhilarating whitewater rafting experience. Feel the excitement build as you tackle challenging rapids, bouncing over waves and navigating through narrow channels. Expert guides will lead you through the most thrilling sections of the river, ensuring a safe yet heart-pounding adventure. Bring your friends or make new ones as you share laughs, high-fives, and memories on this unforgettable journey down the rapids.
